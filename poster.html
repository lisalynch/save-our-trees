Save Our Neighbourhood

Irish Rail plans to build a workers' access road through the private gardens in Ballyrichard, felling mature trees and increasing round-the-clock traffic on Waterrock road.
⏰ DEADLINE TO OBJECT TO OPTION 7: FRIDAY 12 JUNE 2026

What is proposed — Option 7

  • Access road cut through private residential gardens, felling mature native oak and ash trees
  • Round-the-clock traffic on Waterrock Road — 200 cars per day, multiple shift changes day and night
  • Drainage infrastructure routed to Castle Rock Avenue — flood risk concerns for Midleton unaddressed

Why we are objecting

Irish Rail assessed eight options for the access road. Most avoid residential properties entirely.


Their own report says Option 7 was chosen on a "narrow" margin.


We support the Cork Area Commuter Rail Programme and the new depot. We are asking Irish Rail to choose a route that does not destroy our neighbourhood.
